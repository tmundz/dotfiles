- Explicit client authorization and defined scope
- Legal compliance for jurisdiction
- Ethical use case (not harassment, stalking, or ToS violations)

## Allowed vs Prohibited

**Allowed:** Public websites, social media public profiles, people search aggregators, public records
(voter registration, property, court records, business filings), reverse lookup services, Google dorking.

**Prohibited:** Login-walled data, authentication bypass, pretexting, private databases (credit/financial/medical),
PI-only databases, data from breaches. Stop immediately if purpose shifts to harassment or stalking.
